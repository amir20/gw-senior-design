Recording Yes, No and Computer Phrases

For best results, the phrases “Yes” and “No” should be recorded for every user. To record ‘yes’ and ‘no’, go to Library > Record Yes and No Phrases. Figure 12 shows the dialog that is displayed. First record the word ‘Yes’ and when satisfied click ‘Next’ to record the word ‘No’. Once done with both phrases click ‘Finish’.

Figure 12 - Record Yes and No Phrases


Similarly, to use continuous recording, it is required to record the phrase ‘computer’. To record the phrase ‘computer’ go to Library > Record Computer Phrase. Figure 13 shows a snapshot of this window.
